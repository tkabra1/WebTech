When I graduate from Babson, I want to continue to run my startups full time. One of my startups is called Climax Entertainment Group. We provide marketing consultancy, event planning, business consultancy, and media services to nightlife companies in Boston and New York.


My other company is VetX. VetX is a mobile and web based application that allows anyone with a smartphone or internet access to be able to skip traveling and waiting in line to see a vet for something that is probably a false alarm. It allows users to submit a request to consult a vet, provide a description and attach photos/videos, and receive an almost instantaneous reply from a certified vet! It also allows the user to read/post in the community section to see what others have to say, as well as a feature to view available vets in their vicinity to perform a house call in more urgent times.
