About Me

When I graduate from Babson College, I want to continue to run my startups full time. One of my startups is called Climax Entertainment Group. We provide marketing consultancy, event planning, business consultancy, and media services to nightlife companies in Boston and New York. My other company is VetX. VetX is a mobile and web based application that allows anyone with a smartphone or internet access to be able to skip traveling and waiting in line to see a vet for something that is probably a false alarm. It allows users to submit a request to consult a vet, provide a description and attach photos/videos, and receive an almost instantaneous reply from a certified vet! It also allows the user to read/post in the community section to see what others have to say, as well as a feature to view available vets in their vicinity to perform a house call in more urgent times.

PetX is the social media side that will be launched alongside. PetX will allow users to build their pet(s)' profiles which can be used in the VetX application also. It will be the first pet-only social media with stories, photos, videos and even a classified section for those looking for services for their pets! With over 4,000 users on our waitlist to be beta users, we are counting down to our very-near launch date! The inspiration for this idea came from my amazing cat named Boop, and you can see how she loves to sleep with me in the photo on the left! She is a rescue that Katherine and I adopted in May from a shelter that was going to put her down. Her previous owners had abused and abandoned her, so we took her in and she soon became a very important part of our little unit!


My Interests

I have been taking photos for over 7 years now and I have worked with various well known wildlife groups, one of them being National Geographic. I spent 2 months in Kenya and South Africa with a National Geographic on an expedition to document the great migration, which is the passing of wildabeasts and zebras from Kenya over to Tanzania. I also enjoy reading tech blogs and used to contribute product and app reviews to certain websites, such as Engadget and Tech Crunch. At these events, I meet a lot of people that work in and enjoy the same technology industry that I work in and plan to work in, so it is a great networking opportunity. My favorite soccer team is Manchester United. I also enjoy flying drones because I really like technology and taking photos, and drones allow me to do both of those.
