Startups need cash so they can hire developers to build their initial product and reach a proof of concept stage before they raise their next round of money. A lot of companies hire the wrong developers/outsource development for their project in an attempt to save money. While the savings can be significant, the quality of the project usually will be substantially lower than that of one build in house. Saving money on development may seem like a good idea in the beginning, however in the long run, the product will most likely need to be rebuilt.

Instead of investing cash into startups, Climax Group invests our reseources: top notch web and mobile developers, marketing experts, extensive startup advisory board, and more in exchange for an equity participation in the company.
