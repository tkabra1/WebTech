Our story began with one scrawny little kitty found at a local animal shelter outside Boston MA. Boop, the cat had been abandoned by her mother and being in need of a lot of care, was chosen to be taken home by two hard working, busy bodies, who desperately needed a stress relieving furball - I mean cat. While the couple worked on nursing Boop back to health and giving her the best care possible, it lead them to wonder why there wasnâ€™t a simple way for them to get in touch with their vet and why clinics were closed during times when full time employees had, well, free time. After research, deliberation, and a few false alarms at the animal hospital, they created VetX to help pet owners breathe easier and live better. Connecting owners and veterinarians, in real time, at the touch of a button.

VetX is a mobile and web based application / platform that allows anyone with a smartphone access to a professional assessment from a licensed veterinarian. This service meets the needs of pet owners who are traveling, whose regular veterinary hospitals are closed, or who have trouble transporting their pets to the clinics. It allows users to submit a request to consult a vet, provide symptoms and attach photos/videos, and receive an almost instantaneous reply from our network of certified vets. It also allows the user to view available vets in the vicinity to perform a house call (feature will not be launched on the 28th but slightly later on) in more urgent times and make an appointment at the nearest veterinary clinic directly without leaving the app. The web platform will allow users and veterinarians to use this from their desktops / laptops as well.
